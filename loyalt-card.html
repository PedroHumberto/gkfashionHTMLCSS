ADHESION

All holders of the GK Fashion Fidelity Card, both credit and agreement, are automatically participating in the program;
POINTS

For each $10,00 in purchases you get 01 point and reserves 04 points (400%) bonus point, but a purchase is required every 60 days to not lose your points;
BONUS

To validate the earned bonus it will be necessary to do a new purchase in the next month. If you don't realize that purchase you will lose de bonus points;
AWARDS

The points can be exchanged for online products in GK Fashion store, you can see them in the awards catalog;
EXCHANGE DEADLINE

The holder may exchange points for products any time, provided that they reach the minimum amount of points. The points can be accumulated for up to 24 months
POINTS CONSULTATION

The points can be consulted at the card statement, sended montlhy to the holder or in the page Card Consult;
BAD DEBT

In case of default, the accumulated points will be available until the regularization;
